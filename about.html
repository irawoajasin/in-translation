what is this site?

freeforms is an experiment using small language models (SLMs) to explore how we articulate and portray emotion. as you look at the image of yourself, think about the emotion you want to convey in the text. hold space and dictate it to the system that will then generate a poem based on how hundreds of people online depict that emotion through poetry. click 'S' to save your generated poem.
